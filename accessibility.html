Dive into Digital Inclusion: My Accessible Web Wonderland

Hi!

Welcome to our webpage! Today I want to tell you about what measure we took to obtain accessibility and universal design for our web page.

We started by conducting some research about general good practice. This led us to a few reliable sources which we settled on, and based on these we have shaped a webpage we believe to be accessible to a broad audience.

Some of our design choices and general actions towards accesibility are:

  • Following general guidelines such as correct use of white space, high contrast colors, correct use of heading levels and semantic elements and so on.
  • High contrast colors
  • White space
  • Text big enough to be read on any screen by most users.
  • Visible indication of focus.
