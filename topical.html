AI and Chat GPT

Why is everyone so exited about new AI tools and Chat GPT?

Who is Open AI?

Open AI is a company that works and develops artificial intelligence. They are under the Microsoft cooperation and well known in the industry. Open AI have created several tools, for example Chat GPT and Midjourney. These are tools that is based on Open AI’s GPT engine. And are super powerful. Press the image for Open AI's website ->

As mentions earlier, Chat GPT is the most powerful AI tool on the market. But what exactly is Chat GPT? Chat GPT is a word generator based on AI technology. Is responds to questions, math problems, calculations and much more. The hard part by utilizing Chat GPT is the prompting. What to write for getting the best answers. The problem here is that, people often don’t write specific enough prompts, so the answer may not provide the correct information or may be false. Third party developers have also used Chat GPT as a development platform. People have created plugins for the GPT program, and can connect to multiple everyday used apps. Examples can be, internet connection, Google calendar, outlook and blackboks AI.
